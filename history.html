April 19, 1957

McClure's Dairy Isle - Middleport

McClure's Dairy Isle at the corner of Locust and Fourth Street in Middleport opened for business. A walk up store with two serving windows, specializing in soft ice cr√®me desserts, the building design and trademark cone on the roof were unique to the area. In 1984, the building was remodeled to include inside seating, the name was changed to McClure's Restaurant, and more items were added to the menu. Manager is Bob McClure.

1979

McClure's 3 in One Restaurant - Pomeroy

An A-frame building was the home of McClure's 3 in One Restaurant overlooking the Ohio River on East Main Street, Pomeroy, Ohio which opened in 1979. To accommodate growth, the company constructed a brick building next door, moved the restaurant into it, changed the name to McClure's Restaurant. Manager is Jim McClure.

1988

McClure's Restaurant - Gallipolis

In 1988, McClure's Restaurant on Jackson Pike in Gallipolis opened with the same menu selections and drive-thru service as the Middleport and Pomeroy stores. The menu has since expanded to include more items. Manager is Bill McClure.
