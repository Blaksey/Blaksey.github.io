Our History

A journey of quality food and service spanning over seven decades

1950

The Beginning

McClure's Restaurant was founded by John McClure in downtown. Starting with just a small counter and a few stools, John had a vision to serve quality fast food with a personal touch.

1965

Expansion Begins

Due to overwhelming popularity, McClure's opened its second location on the West Side. The menu expanded to include more items, but the commitment to quality remained unchanged.

1978

Family Tradition Continues

John's son, Michael McClure, joined the business and brought fresh ideas while maintaining the family's dedication to excellent food and service.

1990

Third Location Opens

The East Side location opened, bringing McClure's to even more families and communities. The restaurant became a local landmark and gathering place.

2000

New Millennium, Same Values

McClure's celebrated 50 years in business. The restaurant modernized its facilities while keeping the classic recipes and warm atmosphere that customers loved.

2024

Today and Beyond

Now in its third generation of family ownership, McClure's continues to serve the community with the same dedication to quality and service that John McClure established over 70 years ago. We're excited about the future while honoring our past.
