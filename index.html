About Us

Established in 1957, McClure's Restaurant is a family-owned business that has been serving the southeastern Ohio community for over six decades. Our menu features an array of delicious homemade soups, hotdogs, fried foods, specialty ice-creams, and over 30 flavors of milkshakes. McClure's Restaurant offers more than just food - it's an experience!
